Full ownership transfers to the client upon full payment. The designer retains the right to display the project in their portfolio.

The agreed project price covers the initial build and delivery only. Any changes, updates, additions, or edits requested after the website goes live are not included and will be handled under a monthly retainer.

Post-launch updates include (but are not limited to): text changes, image swaps, adding new pages, layout adjustments, and plugin/platform updates. The client must be on an active retainer to request changes. Work outside the retainer scope will be quoted separately.
